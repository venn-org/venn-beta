01 The idea

Two circles. One overlap.

Most apps match you on rent and location. Venn matches you on the rhythm of life — schedules, standards, and the small habits that make or break a flatshare.

You — your lifestyle, budget, and deal-breakers.

Them — someone with their own rhythm, habits, and non-negotiables.

The overlap — where you're compatible enough to actually share a home.

02 Features

Everything you need to find the one.

Smart tools designed to eliminate the awkwardness and uncertainty of finding a flatmate.

Matching

Lens Assessment

Our proprietary quiz goes beyond hobbies — it reads your domestic personality: sleep rhythm, tidiness standards, guest policy, and noise tolerance.

Trust

Verified Profiles

Every user is ID-verified with student/LinkedIn background checks. You'll know who you're meeting before the first message.

Insights

Overlap View

Before you message anyone, see exactly where your routines sync — and where they don't. No more bathroom traffic jams or passive-aggressive notes.

City

Area-smart search

Filter by area, commute time, and budget — not just pin codes. Venn knows that Bandra and ₹25,000/mo mean different things to different people.
